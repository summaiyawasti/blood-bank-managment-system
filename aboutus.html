What the system do?

This project is an innovative approach to address global health.Khoon is an initiative with an aim to connect blood donors with blood seekers.Thousands of Pakistanis die annually due to the blood loss. People run in search of compatible blood group while their loved ones breathe their last. Local blood banks go empty and deliveries are just a minute late to save precious lives.

Our organization keeps a complete record of blood donors for emergency cases. Moreover, our user-friendly site allows the needy to get in touch with the potential donors. With our efficient services, we make sure the recipients acquire blood in no time.
